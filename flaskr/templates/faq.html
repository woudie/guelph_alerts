Q: What is Guelph Alerts?

A: Guelph Alerts is an application designed to allow students to be sign up to be alerted when sections in the course they want open up

Q: How does Guelph Alerts work?

A:
  1. Use the homepage to search for courses
  2. Add those courses to the course cart
  3. After all courses are added, go to the course cart to fill in your email and submit your courses
  4. Sit back, relax, and wait to be alerted via email!

Q: How many courses can I get an alert on?

A: At the moment, I have limited it to 5 per person due to a restriction imposed by webadvisor

Q: How long will my courses be monitored?

A: At the moment, the period is set to 2 weeks. You can re-register them after the 2 week period as much as you want.

Q: Will I get registered in the courses?

A: Unfortunately no. For this, we would need you uoguelph username and password. Also, I'm not sure if it goes against UoG policies

Q: Is this guaranteed to get me into the course I want?

A: Unfortunately it is not. In some cases, the section could fill up before you login and register

Q: Why am I not getting notifications?

A: This project was a practice project, so there may be some bugs. Email me at stwoudie@gmail.com if you notice any issues and I will look into them!
